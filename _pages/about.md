I am **Zijie Song**, currently affiliated with the **School of Data Science and Statistics, Anhui University**. I received my Ph.D. degree in **Computer Science and Technology** from **Hefei University of Technology** in 2025, under the supervision of **Prof. Richang Hong**. My research interests include **multimedia technology**, **cross-modal understanding**, and their applications.

I have participated in several national research projects, including the **National Key Research and Development Program of China**, the **Key Program of the National Natural Science Foundation of China**, the **Joint Fund Project of the National Natural Science Foundation of China**, and the **General Program of the National Natural Science Foundation of China**.

I have published more than ten papers in high-level international conferences and journals, including **IEEE Transactions on Multimedia**, **NeurIPS**, and **IJCAI**. I also serve as a reviewer for more than 20 international journals and conferences, such as **IEEE Transactions on Multimedia**, **ACM Transactions on Multimedia Computing, Communications, and Applications**, and **ICME**. I am a member of the **CSIG Technical Committee on Multimedia**. I received the **ChinaMM 2025 Best Poster Award** and a **Best Paper Nomination**.
